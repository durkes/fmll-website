WE ARE FM LAWN & LANDSCAPE

We are your lawn and landscape professionals, headquartered in Lawrence, Kansas. We take pride in our professionals' creativity and craftsmanship. With talented staff and state of the art equipment, FM Lawn & Landscape is recognized as a leader throughout the Midwest.
