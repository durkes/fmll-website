Service Overview

The success of your landscape depends primarily on two things: sunlight and water. The Midwest heat and fluctuations in rainfall can make it difficult for your landscape to look its best throughout the summer months. Adding irrigation to your landscape alleviates this problem.

Design and Planning

Our professional irrigation technicians can assist you in developing a design which will ensure your lawn will remain a lush green and your plants will thrive, even in the hottest conditions.

Installation

Our experienced professionals have performed hundreds of installs throughout the Lawrence area, including many campus facilities.

Maintenance

Our team is on call day or night should an emergency occur. We will promptly respond to your irrigation maintenance needs.

Irrigation Systems

We use a variety of sprinkler systems to fit customer needs. From Hunter to Ewing, our professionals can handle any system.
